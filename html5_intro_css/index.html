About

This site will describe the differences between the four anteater species (order Pilosa, suborder Vermilingua), share species related to anteaters (magnorder Xenartha), and display pictures of each species. Here at Anteater Facts, we hope to educate folks about anteaters for the benefit of conservation. Specifically giant anteaters are vulnerable with around 5,000 left in the wild. Ways to help with conservation will be presented throughout the website.
